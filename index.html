ABOUT TED

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. Started as a four-day conference in California 30 years ago, TED has grown to support its mission with multiple initiatives. The two annual TED Conferences invite the world’s leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes or less. Many of these talks are then made available, free, at TED.com. TED speakers have included Bill Gates, Jane Goodall, Elizabeth Gilbert, Sir Richard Branson, Nandan Nilekani, Philippe Starck, Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala, Sal Khan and Daniel Kahneman. The annual TED Conference takes place each spring in Vancouver, British Columbia, along with the TEDActive simulcast event in nearby Whistler. TED has established the annual TED Prize, where exceptional individuals with a wish to change the world get help translating their wishes into action; TEDx, which supports individuals or groups in hosting local, self- organized TED-style events around the world, and the TED Fellows program, helping world-changing innovators from around the globe to amplify the impact of their remarkable projects and activities.



ABOUT TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx (where x = independently organized TED event) The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized (Subject to certain rules and regulations).

Shannon Olson
Chemical Ecologist
She is a chemical ecologist who is passionate about understanding the communication between organisms within India's diverse ecosystems. In turn, she is dedicated to bringing people together across various sectors of society to better appreciate, sustain, and listen to our natural world. She is currently the Director of the EChO Network, a social innovation partnership steered by the Principal Scientific Adviser to the Government of India.
A Fulbright Scholar, Ramanujan and INK Fellow, Shannon’s research has been featured by TEDx, Syntalk, Sci-Illustrate, Dublin Science Gallery, V&A London, Chemical and Engineering News, and the DST Science Express train.
Ambika Hiranandani
Lawyer & Activist
Ambika Hiranandani is an environmental and animal rights activist and a lawyer. She is a member of the Maharashtra State Animal Welfare Board and a member of the Society for Prevention of Cruelty to Animals, Mumbai.She has been actively involved in the movement for over ten years. She has written several articles on the inhumane and unregulated practices against animals in India and has continued to remain instrumental in the fight against animal cruelty.
She is currently the government and legal specialist for The Good Food Institute, India. She supports the GFI in its efforts to build thought leadership in the plant-based and cell-based meat, eggs and dairy sector.
Sudip Ghose
MD,VIP Industries Limited
Touted as a dynamo by colleagues and employees alike, Sudip Ghose is a man of relentless energy and always has a positive approach towards his work. With over two decades worth of experience, fourteen of which have been spent in positions of leadership, he is known for his "never say die" attitude.
He is currently the Managing Director of VIP Industries Limited and has worked with Samsonite South Asia, Onida, Whirlpool India, Reliance and Bausch & Lomb.
Saptarshi Prakash
Digital Experience Designer
A graduate of IIT Madras, Saptarshi Prakash has worked with some incredibly well-known startups of India like Housing.com and Zeta India and has rehauled both websites from the ground up. He is currently the Product Design Manager at Swiggy, India’s largest food ordering and delivery platform. He has also given over fifty UX talks and has held workshops at various universities and companies and has written multiple articles on designing.
Chitra Vishwanath
Architect
Chitra Vishwanath, an architect based in Bangalore is the principal architect at Biome Environmental Solutions. She studied for a diploma in civil engineering from Nigeria and a Bachelor of Architecture degree from the CEPT University in Ahmedabad. She has been running her own architectural firm since 1991, working with other architects on many projects in India and Africa. Biome is making major strides in ecological architecture especially in urban settings. In a world where conventional architecture and construction practices do much harm to the environment, firms like Biome strive to bring in a holistic ecosystem-based approach. Biome has been involved in more than 700 projects of various scales and contexts encompassing ecological construction, water harvesting and sanitation structures with specific relevance to the ecology of the sites
Seema Tomar
Trap Shooter
If breaking stereotypes were a target, Seema Tomar seems to be hitting the bullseye. Hailing from Johri, a village in Uttar Pradesh, Seema was expected to marry and become a housewife. However, as fate would have it, her aptitude for shooting, passed down to her by her mother, Prakashi Tomar, famously known as 'Revolver Dadi', an award-winning shooter herself, began to bloom. She started accompanying her mother and aunt to the shooting range and there was no looking back. Seema initially shot with an air rifle, consequently switching to a shotgun and currently specializes in trap shooting. Aged 37, she is the only Indian woman to win the Silver Medal for the shotgun event, at the 'International Shooting Sports Federation'. She has gone forth to secure 6 gold medals and 2 silver medals in various editions of the National Shooting Championship's Trap events. She has also acquired 2 bronze medals in the Asian Clay Shooting Championship's as well as participated in the Commonwealth Games. Alongside her mother, she still continues to run a shooting range in her village where many girls come to learn.
Pavan Rao
Dancer & Actor
Pavan Rao is an actor with a passion for dancing who was crowned second runners up in the Peter England Mr. India 2017, with confidence and valor being his trademark qualities. He was a part of the Fictitious Group, which won the title of 'India’s Got Talent Season 3'. Pavan has danced along with the likes of Salman Khan, Prabhudeva and Shruti Hassan and acted alongside Varun Dhawan and Shraddha Kapoor. He has also performed in shows like 'Nach Baliye' and 'Jhalak Dikhla Ja', and gave a special performance in 'Boogie Woogie'. The talent of the Fictitious Group was acknowledged by choreographer and director, Remo D’souza, and the group featured in the movie ABCD. The sequel was based on the real-life story of the dance group and he starred as one of the actors in ABCD2. After that, he then became a part of the crew 'Kings United India', formed by his mentor. Despite being diagnosed with Hepatitis just before the World HipHop Championship in 2015, he went on to win the bronze medal. His hunger for the gold didn't stop, and in 2019, he was crowned champion with a perfect score receiving praise from Jennifer Lopez and a cash prize of one million dollars.
Dr. Srinivasan Sundarrajan
Scientist & Professor
Dr.Srinivasan Sundarrajan is a reputed Aerospace and Missile Scientist who has worked through all the phases of missile design, production and development of prestigious missile systems like the Brahmos, Agni, Prithvi, Akash, Nag and Thrishul of the Defence Research and Development Organisation. An AICTE Distinguished Professor, Dr.Sundarrajan has made significant contribution to curriculum development, integrating emerging areas of technology. He serves as a member on the boards of ISRO, DAE and CSIR. He has as edited two engineering handbooks and published more than eighty technical papers. He joined as Director of NIT Trichy and has played a vital role in transforming the institute. Currently Dr,Sundarrajan works as a Higher Education & Defence Aerospace consultant and provides strategic consultancy to industries in the areas of defence, aerospace and hi-tech products.
Anubha Maneshwar
Entrepreneur
Anubha Maneshwar is a young entrepreneur, founding director of Girlscript Foundation which is India's biggest tech education organisation operating in 56 cities across India and has impacted nearly 30,000 students in just 2.5 years of its inception. The organization has received Elastic Search Cause Awards 2019 at Sydney Australia. She is a speaker at Tedx, PyCon North America, WWTech Summit Washington DC and more than 150+ other national and international events. She is recipient of Indian Women Leadership Excellence Award for Education, 35 Under 35 Best Startup in Education, India's Top 100 Young Leaders Award and Top 8 across the globe for Technology Playmaker Award by Booking.com at London UK. Recently, she has qualified to semi finals of Forbes Asia 30 Under 30 and is hoping to make the final cut in March 2020 for social entrepreneurship category. GirlScript is a non profit company open for all organisations and supports beginners in technology and programming. It supports diversity by reserving 50% seats for women & lgbtq in all it's programs worldwide. Their aim is to spread awareness about opportunities in tier two & tier three cities and introduce tech content that is helpful for everyone
Akriti Khatri
Private Investigator
Akriti Khatri is a detective who began the Venus Detective Agency, her very own private investigative agency. Born and raised in Delhi, Akriti completed a Bachelor’s degree in Science and an MBA from Delhi University. Preferring to call herself an 'information gatherer' rather than an investigator, she has had a phenomenal success rate of 85%! Branched out agency across several states in India are symbols of her success. More importantly, however, they are also staunch replies to everyone who told her that she was incapable of succeeding in such a dangerous and male dominated profession. But she took no heed and continued on doing what she loves. She has handled scores of high-level investigation cases. With branches in eight cities, and over 80 employees working under her full time, she's definitely reached the pinnacle of success. And nowadays, she also trains young college students in the art of being good detectives.
